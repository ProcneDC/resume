Background

I'm currently a designer and web developer at gcoop cooperative. I earned my Bachelors in Graphic Design at the University of Buenos Aires and I'm a member of the Design and Gender Studies Department of FADU-UBA as teaching assistant. For many years I was a beneficiary of the sarmiento scholarship: a financial aid (res. 289-15) that allowed me to finish my degree, it makes me happy being able to return something and stay in touch with the university.

As a front-end developer, I enjoy bridging the gap between development and design — combining my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create beautiful interfaces. My goal is to always build applications that are scalable while providing engaging, pixel-perfect user experiences.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably drawing, birdwatching or kayaking.
